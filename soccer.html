HEADLINES

Modric Wins Ballon D'Or

The honor for the top player in men's soccer was awarded on Monday to Luka Modric, Croatian midfielder for Real Madrid. Modric's award ends a streak of ten consecutive years where either Lionel Messi or Cristiano Ronaldo took home the hardware, but it's hard to argue that Modric didn't deserve it this time. After helping lead Real Madrid to both the Champions League and La Liga titles in 2018, Modric captained a Croatia side that shocked many in their run to the World Cup Finals. While Croatia fell to France in the last game of the tournament, Modric took home the top honor of the competition, the Golden Ball for best player. Now with the Ballon D'Or to pair that trophy with, Modric's 2018 season is etched in the history books.
